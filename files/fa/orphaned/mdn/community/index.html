The MDN Web Docs site is more than a wiki: It's a community of developers working together to make MDN an outstanding resource for developers who use open Web technologies.

We'd love it if you contribute to MDN, but we'd love it even more if you participate in the MDN community.Â Here's how to get connected, in three easy steps:

  1. Create an MDN account.
  2. Join in conversations.
  3. Follow what's happening.
